About

Freshminlifestyle

Freshmin Lifestyle is a multi-media art/lifestyle brand dedicated to showcasing artist of all genres of art. Originally created in 2012 solely as an apparel brand, FreshMIN has since evolved into a brand that is about giving artist a platform and a place they can go to immerse themselves in the things that interest them. WE are a brand that is always seeking out new content to add to our growing culture and to give to the people that consider themselves as being a part of our FreshMIN family. As we grow into a hub for artist and art we only hope that our FreshMIN supporters grows with us. Live Free, Live Fresh!
